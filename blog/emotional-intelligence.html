What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence (EQ) is the ability to notice, understand, and manage our own feelings, as well as recognize and respond to the feelings of others.

How is EQ different to IQ?

IQ is about thinking skills, problem solving, and logic. EQ is about people skills, self-awareness, and managing emotions. Both are useful, but they focus on different areas.

Why is EQ important?

EQ helps us work well with others, handle stress, and build stronger relationships. In jobs and everyday life, high EQ often makes a bigger difference than high IQ.
