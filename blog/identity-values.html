Identity & Values

Ethical Decision

During a university team project, one of my teammates was not participating as expected. Instead of ignoring the issue, I reached out to him to understand why. I discovered that he was unsure of what to do and was too shy to contact the lecturer for guidance. I decided to guide him step by step on what needed to be done and how to improve his part. By supporting him, our team was able to complete the project successfully. This experience taught me the importance of empathy, clear communication, and taking ethical responsibility to help others when challenges arise

Culture & Influence

Growing up in Korea has shaped who I am today. I learned the importance of respect, family, and community from a young age. Korean culture values hard work and perseverance, which helps me stay focused when facing challenges. Watching my parents and elders taught me patience, humility, and the importance of helping others. These lessons guide how I make decisions, work with people, and take responsibility in both my personal life and learning.

Strengths & Limitations

One of my strengths is that I always make sure I fully understand the content before moving on. This helps me build a strong foundation and ensures I don’t miss important details. However, English is my second language, so sometimes I find it hard to communicate my thoughts in depth. I also feel a bit shy when asking for help, especially in group settings. These challenges mean I sometimes take longer to clarify things, but they also push me to be patient, reflective, and careful in my learning. By recognizing these limitations, I can plan ahead, ask for help when needed, and collaborate more effectively with others.

Teamwork & Tension

I think in a team environments, disagreements are normal. For example, during a project, my peer and I had different ideas about how to approach a task. Instead of insisting on my own way, we asked the lecturer and teaching assistant for guidance on the best approach. By considering their feedback, we adjusted our work to meet the expectations and successfully completed the project. This experience taught me the importance of listening, seeking guidance when needed, and being flexible. In the future, I would continue to communicate openly and involve others when disagreements arise to ensure the team works effectively together.
